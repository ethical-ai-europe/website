## Why This Matters for You

**Education AI is classified as high-risk under the EU AI Act.** When AI systems determine access to education, evaluate learning outcomes, or assess students, they must meet strict requirements for transparency, fairness, and human oversight.

As an educator, you're in a unique position. You can:

- **Protect students** by asking the right questions before tools are adopted
- **Model responsible AI use** by being transparent about how you use these tools
- **Advocate for better practices** within your institution
- **Prepare students** for a world where AI literacy is essential

The decisions you make today shape how the next generation understands and interacts with AI. That's both a responsibility and an opportunity.
