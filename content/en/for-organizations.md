## Ethics and Compliance: Two Sides of the Same Coin

Some organizations treat ethical AI and regulatory compliance as separate concerns‚Äîone for the philosophy department, one for the lawyers. **That's a mistake.**

The EU AI Act is built on ethical foundations:

- **Transparency** requirements exist because people deserve to know when AI affects them
- **Human oversight** mandates exist because algorithms shouldn't make life-changing decisions alone
- **Bias testing** requirements exist because AI shouldn't discriminate

When you build ethical AI systems, you're already doing most of what compliance requires. And when you follow compliance requirements, you're implementing the practical aspects of AI ethics.

**The practical upshot:** Don't have separate "ethics" and "compliance" initiatives. Build one program that does both.

## Common Questions

### "We're small‚Äîdoes the EU AI Act even apply to us?"

Yes, if you're deploying AI systems that affect EU residents. Size doesn't exempt you from the rules, though your obligations scale with your risk level. Most SMEs using standard AI tools have minimal obligations.

### "We just use tools‚Äîwe're not building AI."

That's the situation for most organizations. You still have responsibilities, but they're less extensive than for AI developers. The key is knowing which tools you're using and understanding their risk levels.

### "This seems complicated. Where do we start?"

Start with an inventory. What AI tools and systems does your organization use? That simple question‚Äîanswered honestly‚Äîgives you the foundation for everything else.

## Key Timelines

The EU AI Act is being phased in:

| Date | What's Required |
|------|-----------------|
| **August 2024** | Act enters into force |
| **February 2025** | Prohibited AI practices banned |
| **August 2025** | General-purpose AI rules apply |
| **August 2026** | Most high-risk AI requirements apply |
| **August 2027** | Full application to all AI systems |

**Don't wait until the deadlines.** Organizations that start preparing now will have an easier transition.
