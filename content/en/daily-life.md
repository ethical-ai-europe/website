## Questions to Ask in Any Context

When you suspect AI is involved in decisions that affect you, start with these questions:

**1. "Was AI or an automated system involved in this decision?"**
You have the right to know. Ask directly.

**2. "How did the system reach this conclusion?"**
You're entitled to an explanation, not just a result.

**3. "Can a human review this decision?"**
For significant decisions, you can request human oversight.

**4. "What data was used, and was it accurate?"**
Decisions are only as good as the data they're based on.

**5. "How can I challenge this if I disagree?"**
Know your options for appeal or complaint.

## Your Rights Apply Everywhere

Whether you're a student, patient, job seeker, or citizen interacting with government:

‚úì **You have the right to know** when AI is being used in decisions about you
