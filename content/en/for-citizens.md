## 3 Things You Can Do Today

1. **Ask one question.** Next time AI might be involved in a decision about you, ask: "Was any automated system or algorithm involved in this decision?"

2. **Check your children's school.** Ask what AI tools are being used, what data is collected, and how long it's kept.

3. **Review one policy.** Look at your employer's IT policy or a service's privacy policy. Are they being transparent about AI use?
